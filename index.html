About the synctuario Attendance System

The synctuario Attendance System is a robust, easy-to-use platform designed for schools and companies to automate attendance tracking using RFID technology. It combines hardware and software to provide a seamless, real-time attendance monitoring experience.

Key Features:

  • Effortlessly register and track attendance with RFID cards or tags scanned via ESP32 devices.
  • View live attendance data with detailed reports and analytics accessible to admins and teachers.
  • Different access levels for admins and teachers ensure security and proper permissions.
  • Supports English and French to cater to diverse user bases.
  • Flexible monthly and yearly subscription plans for schools and companies of all sizes.
  • Get a 15-day free trial when you sign up.
  • Works for both schools and companies — fully adaptable.
  • Your data is securely saved and available for future use.
  • When an admin registers, a unique API key is generated for secure device communication.
  • Student or employee UID changes can only be made by an admin or CEO, and the ID owner will be notified.
  • Attendance records can be exported in Excel format.

How It Works:

Students or employees are registered with unique RFID cards. When a card is scanned on the ESP32 reader, the system logs the attendance with timestamps. Admins and teachers can monitor attendance live, generate reports, and manage users via a secure web interface.
